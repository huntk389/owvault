### **Allium-Free Savory Seasoning Blend**

**Yield:** ~½ cup  
**Shelf Life:** 3–6 months airtight in a cool, dry space

**Ingredients:**
- 2 tbsp smoked paprika (or sweet if preferred)
- 1 tbsp ground cumin
- 1 tbsp dried thyme
- 1 tbsp ground coriander
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- 1 tbsp nutritional yeast (adds umami)
- 1½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp mustard powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp ground fennel (optional, adds depth)
- ½ tsp dried lemon peel or sumac (for brightness)
- ½ tsp celery seed or ground lovage (for savory punch)
- Pinch of cayenne or Aleppo pepper (optional heat)
- Salt to taste when using (leave unsalted for flexibility)

**Instructions:**
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Whisk well or pulse in a spice grinder for a more uniform texture.
3. Store in a labeled airtight jar or spice tin.

---

**Flavor Notes:**
- **Umami + Brightness:** Nutritional yeast and lemon/sumac help replicate that "rounded" flavor you’d typically get from alliums.
- **Savory Depth:** Celery seed, cumin, and fennel bring in hearty notes without overpowering.
- **Custom Ideas:** Add rosemary or sage for a more herbaceous blend, or smoked salt for extra BBQ vibes.
