### 1. **Grilled Chicken with Avocado Salsa**
   **Ingredients:**
   - 4 chicken breasts
   - 2 avocados, diced
   - 1 tomato, diced
   - 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
   - 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
   - 1 lime, juiced
   - Salt and pepper to taste

   **Instructions:**
   1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a little olive oil. Grill over medium-high heat until cooked through (about 6-7 minutes per side).
   2. While the chicken grills, prepare the salsa by mixing the avocado, tomato, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice in a bowl.
   3. Serve the grilled chicken topped with the avocado salsa.

### 2. **Zucchini Noodles with Pesto**
   **Ingredients:**
   - 4 zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
   - 2 cups fresh basil
   - 1/4 cup pine nuts
   - 2 garlic cloves
   - 1/4 cup olive oil
   - Salt and pepper to taste

   **Instructions:**
   1. In a food processor, blend the basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper until smooth.
   2. Sauté the zucchini noodles in a pan over medium heat for 2-3 minutes until tender.
   3. Toss the zucchini noodles with the pesto and serve immediately.

### 3. **Crispy Baked Salmon with Roasted Vegetables**
   **Ingredients:**
   - 4 salmon fillets
   - 1 tablespoon olive oil
   - 1 teaspoon paprika
   - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
   - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   - Assorted vegetables (e.g., carrots, Brussels sprouts, and bell peppers)

   **Instructions:**
   1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
   2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with paprika, garlic powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
   3. Arrange the vegetables on another baking sheet and season with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
   4. Bake both the salmon and vegetables for 15-20 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

---

### 4. **Eggplant Lasagna**
   **Ingredients:**
   - 2 large eggplants, sliced into thin rounds
   - 1 pound ground turkey or beef
   - 1 cup tomato sauce (sugar-free)
   - 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
   - 1/4 cup olive oil
   - Salt and pepper to taste

   **Instructions:**
   1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
   2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Brown the ground meat, then stir in the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
   3. Lay the eggplant slices on a baking sheet and roast for 10 minutes on each side.
   4. In a baking dish, layer the roasted eggplant slices with the meat sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of eggplant on top.
   5. Bake for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and heated through.

---

### 5. **Coconut Chicken Tenders**
   **Ingredients:**
   - 4 chicken breasts, cut into strips
   - 1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
   - 1/2 cup almond flour
   - 1 egg, beaten
   - Salt and pepper to taste

   **Instructions:**
   1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
   2. In a shallow bowl, mix the shredded coconut, almond flour, salt, and pepper.
   3. Dip each chicken strip into the beaten egg, then coat with the coconut mixture.
   4. Arrange the coated chicken strips on the baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and crispy.

These recipes are flavorful, simple to prepare, and cater to dairy, grain, and sugar-free diets. You can always adjust the seasonings based on preference!

### **Fajita-Style Steak or Chicken Lettuce Wraps**  

#### **Ingredients:**  
- 1 lb skirt steak or chicken breast, sliced into thin strips  
- 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced  
- 1 red onion, sliced  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 tsp chili powder  
- 1 tsp cumin  
- 1 tsp smoked paprika  
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder  
- 1/2 tsp onion powder  
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)  
- Salt & black pepper to taste  
- Juice of 1 lime  
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped  
- Butter lettuce or romaine leaves (for wraps)  

#### **Instructions:**  
1. **Marinate the Meat**: In a bowl, toss the steak or chicken with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne (if using), salt, and pepper. Let it sit for at least 15 minutes (or overnight for more flavor).  
2. **Cook the Protein**: Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the steak (about 3-4 minutes per side for medium) or chicken (5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked). Remove and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing thinly.  
3. **Sauté the Veggies**: In the same pan, add a bit more olive oil if needed and sauté the bell peppers and onions for about 5 minutes until tender but slightly crisp. Squeeze lime juice over them.  
4. **Assemble & Serve**: Place the sliced meat and sautéed veggies into lettuce leaves. Garnish with fresh cilantro and extra lime juice.  

#### **Optional Additions:**  
- **Avocado slices** or guacamole  
- **Salsa fresca** (diced tomatoes, onions, and cilantro)  
- **Cashew or coconut cream** as a dairy-free "sour cream" alternative  

This gives you all the bold fajita flavors while keeping it clean and grain-free!

[2025-04-03 18:58:50 UTC] Assistant: A **scrambled eggs and bacon breakfast** can easily be modified to be dairy, grain, and sugar-free while keeping it delicious and satisfying. Here's a simple recipe you can try:

### **Scrambled Eggs with Bacon (Dairy, Grain & Sugar-Free)**

#### **Ingredients:**
- 6 large eggs
- 4-6 slices of bacon (check that it's sugar-free if you're avoiding sugar)
- 1 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil (for scrambling the eggs)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: Fresh herbs like parsley, chives, or cilantro (for garnish)

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Cook the Bacon:**
   - In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy, about 4-5 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the bacon.
   - Remove the bacon from the pan and drain it on a paper towel-lined plate. Save a little of the bacon grease in the skillet for cooking the eggs for added flavor (or discard if you prefer).
   
2. **Scramble the Eggs:**
   - Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and whisk until well combined.
   - Heat a bit of olive or avocado oil in the same skillet over medium-low heat (or use the reserved bacon grease).
   - Pour in the eggs and gently stir occasionally, cooking until they’re scrambled to your desired consistency (about 2-3 minutes).
   
3. **Serve:**
   - Crumble the bacon and sprinkle it on top of the scrambled eggs, or serve it on the side.
   - Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

#### **Optional Sides:**
- **Avocado slices** for extra creaminess and healthy fats.
- **Sautéed vegetables** like spinach, bell peppers, or mushrooms.
- **Fresh salsa** for a little kick!

This breakfast is full of protein, healthy fats, and is easy to make without any dairy, grains, or added sugar. Enjoy!
